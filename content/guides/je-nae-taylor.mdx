- Kaba, M. (2021). We do this' til we free us: Abolitionist organizing and transforming justice (Vol. 1). Haymarket Books.

## Quote

> “When we think about sustaining life, we know that includes honoring tradition, real relationship building and transcribing lessons, recipes, and pursuits to be shared and sewn into the future. Cultural Organizing is about that life!"
