# A study guide: Josh MacPhee

[Josh MacPhee](https://blog.pmpress.org/authors-artists-comrades/josh-macphee/) is a designer, artist, and archivist, whose publishing output has inspired and supported
fellow artists and activists for close to 3 decades. He is a founding member of the Justseeds Artists’
Cooperative and Interference Archive, based in Brooklyn, NY. Justseeds and Interference Archive are
two of the largest political art, cultural organizing, and social movement production spaces in the U.S.
Interference Archive also maintains a popular history of social movement art in its open access archives.
MacPhee is the author and editor of numerous publications, including Signs of Change: Social Movement
Cultures 1960s to Now; Signal: A Journal of International Political Graphics and Culture; the Encyclopedia
of Political Record Labels; and Graphic Liberation: Image Making and Political Movements. He has
organized the [Celebrate People’s History poster series](https://justseeds.org/project/cph/) since 1998 and has been
designing book covers for publishers for over a decade.
